# Start Here

This repository is a public-safe Market Decision Archive for Maximilion.

## In 5 Seconds

Maximilion does not publish signals.

It builds a reviewable memory of market decisions under risk.

## What To Look For

Look for the pattern:

```text
terrain -> correlation -> conflict check -> stance -> review
```

The signal is not whether a market call was right.

The signal is whether the decision was documented, bounded, reviewable, and later improved.

## Public-Safe Rule

Do not add entries, stops, targets, live order recommendations, account information, private strategy logic, customer material, or sensitive architecture.
