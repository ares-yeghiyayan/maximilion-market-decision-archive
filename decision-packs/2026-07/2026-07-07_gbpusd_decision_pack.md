GBPUSD is being evaluated after sterling recently reached a three-week high against the U.S. dollar. However, the Wall Street Journal reported that U.S. Treasury yields were rising, with the 10-year yield near 4.495% and the two-year near 4.139%, while DXY was slightly firmer.
