# Maximilion Market Decision Framework

## Purpose

This archive is not a signal service.

It is a public-safe Market Decision Archive for recording:

- market context
- risk logic
- correlation exposure
- hidden factor exposure
- conflict checks
- decision stance
- later review

The goal is to prove how Maximilion thinks under uncertainty.

## Core Principle

Maximilion does not ask only:

> Is this trade good?

It asks:

> What decision is justified by the terrain, risk, correlation, mandate, and review evidence?

## 1. Asset Universe

Initial assets:

- XAUUSD
- EURUSD
- GBPUSD
- USDJPY
- USDCAD

No more until the archive becomes repeatable.

## 2. Market Terrain Check

Every Decision Pack must begin with terrain.

```md
Market Regime:
- Trend
- Range
- Volatile
- Crisis
- Dead Market

Liquidity:
- Normal
- Thin
- Dangerous

Volatility:
- Low
- Normal
- High
- Extreme

News Risk:
- Low
- Medium
- High

Execution Risk:
- Low
- Medium
- High
```

Rule:

If terrain is unclear, decision stance must be:

```md
WAIT / DEFENSIVE ONLY
```

## 3. Decision Stance

Choose one:

- No Decision
- Wait
- Bullish Bias
- Bearish Bias
- Defensive Only

A Decision Stance is not execution. It is a recorded posture under uncertainty.

## 4. Defensive Mode Rule

Use Defensive Only when:

- correlations break
- news risk is high
- liquidity is thin
- hidden factor exposure is unclear
- multiple positions depend on the same macro factor
- market regime is crisis or unstable

Allowed:

- reduce exposure
- wait
- hedge
- cancel conflicting ideas
- mark for review

Forbidden:

- add aggressive new exposure
- stack correlated positions
- treat duplicated USD risk as diversification
- ignore hidden factor exposure

## 5. Review Rule

Every Decision Pack needs a review plan:

- 30 days
- 60 days
- 90 days

The review should ask:

- What happened?
- Was the original logic valid?
- Was the risk correctly identified?
- Was the decision stance too aggressive, too defensive, or appropriate?
- What should Maximilion learn?

## One-Line Summary

Maximilion does not publish signals.

Maximilion builds a memory of decisions under risk.
