# Correlation Map

Every Decision Pack must include positive correlation, negative correlation, and hidden factor exposure.

## XAUUSD

Positive Correlation:

- risk-off sentiment
- inflation fear
- geopolitical stress
- weak USD

Negative Correlation:

- strong USD
- rising real yields
- hawkish Fed expectations

Hidden Factor Exposure:

- DXY
- US real yields
- Fed policy
- Treasury yields
- global risk sentiment
