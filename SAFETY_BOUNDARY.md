# Safety Boundary

This archive is public-safe.

It is designed to show decision discipline without exposing private strategy or creating trading-signal behavior.

## This Repository May Contain

- public-safe market context
- terrain classification
- correlation exposure
- hidden factor exposure
- conflict checks
- non-execution decision stance
- risk notes
- later review notes
- framework documents and templates

## This Repository Must Not Contain

- private strategy logic
- execution instructions
- entries
- stop losses
- take profits
- position sizing
- account information
- broker information
- customer data
- sensitive architecture
- live order recommendations
- performance claims

## Non-Execution Rule

A Decision Stance is not a trade instruction.

Allowed stances are:

- No Decision
- Wait
- Bullish Bias
- Bearish Bias
- Defensive Only

These describe decision posture only. They do not authorize execution.

## Public Proof Rule

This archive proves decision discipline, not trading performance.
