# Maximilion Market Decision Archive

Maximilion does not publish signals.

Maximilion builds a reviewable memory of decisions under risk.

This repository is a public-safe archive for Market Decision Packs. It records how market context, terrain, correlation exposure, hidden factors, conflict checks, decision stance, and later review are documented under uncertainty.

It is not a signal service.

It does not publish trade calls, entries, stop losses, take profits, position sizing, or live order recommendations.

## Why This Exists

The goal is to show how Maximilion thinks under uncertainty.

Maximilion does not ask only:

> Is this trade good?

It asks:

> What decision is justified by the terrain, risk, correlation, mandate, and review evidence?

## Initial Asset Universe

The archive starts with a narrow asset universe:

- XAUUSD
- EURUSD
- GBPUSD
- USDJPY
- USDCAD

No more assets should be added until the archive becomes repeatable.

## What A Decision Pack Contains

Each Decision Pack should include:

- market context
- market terrain
- correlation and hidden factor exposure
- bull case
- bear case
- conflict check
- decision stance
- risk notes
- review plan
- future review

## Decision Stance Is Not Execution

A Decision Stance is not an instruction to trade.

It does not include:

- entry
- stop loss
- take profit
- position size
- execution timing
- live recommendation

It only records the current decision posture under uncertainty.

## Public Boundary

This archive proves decision discipline, not trading performance.

Private strategy logic, execution systems, account information, customer data, sensitive architecture, and live order recommendations stay outside this repository.
